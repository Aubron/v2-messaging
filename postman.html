Postman Collection

Follow along with Postman

Postman is an app for easy RESTful API exploration. Use the Builder below to get a "Postman Collection" with a prefilled environment with your Bandwidth credentials.

Assumptions

  • You have downloaded Postman
  • You have your Voice and Messaging API userId, token, secret
  • You have your Phone Number API username and password
  • You have at least one subaccount or site on your Phone Number Dashboard

Fill in the form then Click Run in Postman below to explore our V2 API Collection.
